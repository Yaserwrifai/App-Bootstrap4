Popular Destinations in Palestine

Al-Aqsa Mosque
One of Islam's holiest sites, located on the Haram al-Sharif (Noble Sanctuary).
Church of the Nativity – Bethlehem
Believed to be the birthplace of Jesus Christ. Major pilgrimage site for Christians worldwide. Listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
Hebron (Al-Khalil) – Ibrahimi Mosque
Sacred to Muslims, Jews, and Christians — believed to house the tombs of Abraham, Sarah, Isaac, Jacob, and others. A site of religious and political significance.
